Ashrita Furman

Ashrita Furman is the current holder of the Guinness record for the most records, with over 120 records ranging from creating the largest flower garland to the longest distance cycling underwater. Ashrita has been a student of meditation teacher Sri Chinmoy for over 40 years, and credits meditation and his teachers encouragement for the capacity to break his records. You can read more on his site at ashrita.com
