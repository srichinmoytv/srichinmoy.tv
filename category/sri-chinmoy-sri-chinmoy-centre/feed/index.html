]]> http://www.srichinmoy.tv/5549/secrets-of-the-universe-2/feed/ 0 My New York http://www.srichinmoy.tv/5540/my-new-york/ http://www.srichinmoy.tv/5540/my-new-york/#comments Thu, 09 May 2013 16:50:52 +0000 http://www.srichinmoy.tv/?p=5540 Sri Chinmoy lived in New York from 1964 until his mahasamadhi in 2007. In a short essay entitled “My New York”  Sri Chinmoy wrote about the qualities and strengths which epitomise his adopted home town of New York.

In this video, Antara Prabhat Kalagian, narrates this essay against a backdrop of video footage from New York.

“I admire New York. My eyes are enamoured of her soul’s dynamic beauty. My New York is always astir and bustling. Also, is she marching in gigantic strides. Success emerges before her very eye…”

– Sri Chinmoy,  “My New York“,  America In Her Depths, Agni Press, 1973

 

During his four decades of living in New York, Sri Chinmoy gave regular meditations at the United Nations and was based in Jamaica, Queens.
