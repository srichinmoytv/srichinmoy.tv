Since 1994, spiritual teacher Sri Chinmoy, created innumerable Soul-Bird-Drawings as part of his creative artwork. Sri Chinmoy loved to draw birds, especially during his many travels abroad. In January of 2001, during a vacation in Bali, Sri Chinmoy drew a special series of birds, using a variety of everyday items as the canvas for his birds.
