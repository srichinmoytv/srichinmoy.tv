Many came from Great Britain and two swimmers came all the way from India to this popular Marathon Swim over a distance of 26.4 k from Rapperswil to Zurich. This year’s winner in the main men’s category was David Cech from Brno, Czech Republic; first among the women was Nancy Douglas from Wales. Relive these moments of joy in the water with our summary of the 2007 event.
