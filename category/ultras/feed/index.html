]]> http://www.srichinmoy.tv/564/successful-100-km-race/feed/ 0 22nd Self-Transcendence 12+24 Hour Race, Basel 2010 http://www.srichinmoy.tv/507/22nd-self-transcendence-1224-hour-race-basel-2010/ http://www.srichinmoy.tv/507/22nd-self-transcendence-1224-hour-race-basel-2010/#comments Mon, 31 May 2010 05:00:29 +0000 http://devel.srichinmoy.tv/507/22nd-self-transcendence-1224-hour-race-basel-2010  

On May 8/9, 2010, the Sri Chinmoy Marathon Team organized the 22nd edition of the 12+24 Hour Race in the St. Jakobs Sports Stadium in Basel, Switzerland. Many new runners that joined the group come to Basel regularly – all together 122 from 13 nations.

All races of the Sri Chinmoy Marathon Team use a round course that gives them with the best opportunity to be of service to the runners around the clock with counter, food and medical stations. In addition to this several music groups such as Mountain-Silence and Adesh & Ajita performed at the course for inspiration.

Although the weather was not ideal in the evening, the atmosphere was friendly and enthusiastic until the finish on Sunday noon. First among the men in the 24 hour category was Ludger Robert Boewer with a distance of 238 k and among the women Heike Christ with 208 k, both from Germany.

The fastest 12 hour runners were Thomas Herget (134 k) and Dorothea Pfeffer (115 k), also from Germany.

-Kedar Misani

A record 15 runners from 10 countries entered the event, which is being held for the tenth time. Participants have 51 days to complete the distance. Circling a large city block in Queens, they can run from 6 in the morning until midnight each night.

World record holder for the event, Madhupran Wolfgang Schwerk, has entered once again promising that this will be his last race. Two other previous winners are also participating. For all who finish, just to complete the 3100 mile distance is an incredible achievement.
