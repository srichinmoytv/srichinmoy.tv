12 Runners from 9 countries have come to take part in the longest race in the world, The Self Transcendence 3100 Mile race. This is the 11th year the event has been held in New York City. It began on Sunday June 17th and each athlete has up to 51 days to complete the distance.

You can read more about this year’s record setting race at SriChinmoyRaces.org

- Utpal Marshall
